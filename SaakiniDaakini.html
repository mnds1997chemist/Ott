Saakini Daakini




My List

Play

"Saakini Daakini" is a Telugu action-comedy film that brings a fresh twist to the genre with its engaging storyline and dynamic characters. Directed by Sudheer Varma, the movie is a remake of the South Korean film "Midnight Runners" and features an intriguing plot centered around two female police trainees, played by Regina Cassandra and Nivetha Thomas. Their on-screen chemistry and spirited performances drive the narrative, blending humor with high-octane action sequences. The film explores themes of friendship, resilience, and justice as the protagonists find themselves embroiled in a perilous mission to uncover a kidnapping ring. With its blend of gripping action and lighthearted moments, "Saakini Daakini" has garnered attention for its entertaining take on a buddy-cop adventure, marking a notable addition to contemporary Telugu cinema.
